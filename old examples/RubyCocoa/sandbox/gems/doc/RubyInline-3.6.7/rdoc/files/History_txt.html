3.2.0 / 2004-11-27

  • 8 minor enhancements

      * Better execeptions are thrown on error.
      * Incorporated a bunch of changes from Jonathan Paisley:
              * Generation of inline'd class methods. (c_singleton and c_raw_singleton)
