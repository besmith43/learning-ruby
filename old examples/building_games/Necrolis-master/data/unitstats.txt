#SIGHT_RANGE (TILES)
#ATTACK_RANGE (TILES) (MELEE=1.1)
#MISSILE (ID OF MISSILE GRAPHIC) (NONE)
#ENERGY_COST (ENERGY COST OF UNIT)
#CORPSES_COST (CORPSES COST OF THE UNIT)
#

#HEALTH 2
#DAMAGE 1
#ARMOR 0

#CORPSES_COST 1
